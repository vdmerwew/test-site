The Australian Shepherd Club of the Western Cape

Welcome to the Australian Shepherd Club of the Western Cape, a community built by and for people who love Australian Shepherds!

Our club was founded with a simple mission:

To celebrate, protect, and promote the Australian Shepherd breed.

Photo credit: J.Harris

What We Do

  • Education: Responsible ownership, training, health, and history.
  • Community Events: Agility competitions, meet-ups, workshops.
  • Breed Advocacy: Ethical breeding, rescue, health testing.
  • Sports: Obedience, flyball, rally, conformation shows.
Photo credit: Facebook

Our Commitment

We value:
  • Responsible ownership
  • Breed health and preservation
  • Respect for all Aussie types
  • Support for all experience levels
  • Positive, inclusive community
Photo credit: Facebook

Join Us!

Whether you want to compete, connect, learn, or simply celebrate the joy of living with an Aussie, we’re here for you.

Together, we honor the past, celebrate the present, and protect the future of our remarkable breed.
