Why Join the Australian Shepherd Club?

Celebrate your love for Australian Shepherds with a community that gets it!

  • Meet other Aussie lovers
  • Access expert training and breed advice
  • Enjoy exclusive events and workshops
  • Support breed rescue and education
  • Get member-only perks and discounts

Whether you're a new owner or a seasoned competitor, there's a place for you here!

Image: Facebook

Membership

Join our community of Aussie lovers! Whether you’re a proud Aussie owner, a breeder, a competitor, or an enthusiast, we welcome you to become part of our family. Think of it as your go-to resource for all things Aussie. You'll get to swap stories, share tips, and learn from experienced owners who understand the unique joys (and occasional quirks!) of living with these intelligent and energetic dogs. Plus, you'll stay in the loop about breed-specific events, training opportunities, and important health information.

Types of Membership

  • Ordinary Membership — 1 adult
  • Family Membership — 2 adults + children under 18
  • Honorary Life Membership nominated by Committee — For long-term supporters

Member Benefits

  • Invitations to club events, fun days, and competitions
  • Discounts on training clinics and merchandise
  • Eligibility for awards and recognition programs
  • Voting rights at the Annual General Meeting (for adult members)
